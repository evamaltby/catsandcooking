Cooking Tips: Cats and Cooking

Correct Equipment- pots, pans etc...

Having the correct equipment makes cooking a whole lot easier. Make sure they are easily accessible and clean. Having other equipment like a peeler or a knife-sharpener makes your cooking quicker and more tasty! If you need help deciding which equipment you need, watch this video from Gordon Ramsey.

Good Ingredients

Make sure you get the best ingredients you can afford. Markets are a great place to get good food and cheap prices. If not markets, authentic butchers, fishmongers and bakery's are also great places for hunting good food. To find your local food retailers click here for the UK or here for the USA. Remember your butcher/baker/fishmonger will be happy to advise you and help you get the right ingredients for your dish(es).

Flavour

No matter what you are cooking you always want it to taste good. Seasining with a bit of salt and pepper is a great way to add in a bit of flavour. Chilli, flakes or paste, is also a good way to pack a punch in your dish just make sure that the chilli flavour goes witht the rest of the ingredients. If in doubt dont be scared to check on the internet (click here to visit a website that might help) and raid the cupboards for spices and condiments that might just make your dish.

Keep a Cool Head

Although Gordon Ramsey is one of the worlds greatest chef's, he doesn't always have the best attitude. Keep a cool head in the kitchen to prevent mistakes. But remember that things don't always go right but they normally aren't ruined either. You can search the internet (we recommend: Food Network, BBC GoodFood and Delish), call a friend or use your instinct to fix your dish.
